OCTOBER 6th

1913 Miss Dunn arrived at 4 Crown Terrace . Went for a walk. Beautiful day.

1914 Beautiful day. Helped to stick on flags for Belgium Flag Day.

1915 A complement convoy of 20 men arrived to help us pack.

1916 Dentist at 1.00. Very busy place but very pleasant.

1917 Went for a walk in garden.

1918 Beautiful day. Walked up Thames about two miles.

OCTOBER 7th

1913 Singing lesson and lunch in town with Alice. Brought hat.

1914 Baked cake for Mrs McDougall. Went for walk with Alice.

1915 In kitchen all day, Browne packing up Field Stuff.

1916 Dr Emaly Emile has gone home with jaundice.

1917 Patsy in bed in morning. Very wet day.

1918 Went to Woodleigh, to discuss managed from Flowerdown Fareham .

1918 Stocktaking at Flowerdown Fareham .
